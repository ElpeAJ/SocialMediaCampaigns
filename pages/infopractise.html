Stay Safe Social Media

About Our Campaigns

The online world offers a fantastic way for teenagers to connect with friends, express themselves, and explore new interests. However, it's also important to be aware of the potential risks associated with social media use.

**Stay Safe Social Media** is dedicated to empowering teenagers to navigate the social media landscape safely and responsibly. We run a variety of campaigns focusing on different aspects of online safety, including:

  • Cyberbullying prevention and intervention
  • Online privacy and security awareness
  • Healthy social media use habits
  • Recognizing and avoiding online scams
  • Managing digital reputation

Our Vision

We envision a future where teenagers can enjoy the positive aspects of social media while being equipped with the knowledge and skills to protect themselves online.
